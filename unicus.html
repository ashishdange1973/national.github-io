Summer Olympiad Exam -- Unicus Olympiads.

Unicus Olympiads are India's first Summer Olympiads. These exams take place in May and July 2021. The students are burdened with Olympiad exams based on curriculum that's not even complete by the time the exams are conducted. Unicus Olympiads assesses students' practical application of concepts based on what they have studied.
